# Disclaimer and Responsible Use

Structure Hunter is a research, survey, and educational tool. Please read this before using it.

## Findings are candidates, not conclusions

The program identifies locations where a physical structure appears to exist without a corresponding official record. These are **leads for further investigation**, not proof of anything. A flagged location may be:

- A perfectly ordinary building whose address point happens to sit far from its footprint.
- A structure that simply hasn't been mapped or registered yet — common in rural areas with sparse data.
- A data artifact, a non-building structure, or noise.

Always confirm findings against current aerial imagery and, where appropriate, through lawful on-the-ground verification with the permission of the property owner.

## Data quality varies

- **LiDAR** quality depends on the survey project: point density and flight season matter. Leaf-off (winter) scans can see under bare deciduous canopy; summer scans largely cannot; evergreen cover blocks the laser year-round.
- **Address and footprint coverage** is uneven across regions and sources. "No record" can mean "not yet mapped" rather than "deliberately hidden."

## Legitimate use only

This tool is intended for purposes such as:

- Historical and archaeological survey
- Research on your own property
- Land management, conservation, and planning
- Education and learning about geospatial analysis

**Do not** use this tool to trespass, harass, stalk, surveil, or otherwise target individuals, or to violate any privacy, property, or other laws. Respect property rights and local regulations at all times.
